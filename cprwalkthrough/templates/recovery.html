CPR Walkthrough

Place in Recovery Position

1. Extend the victim's left arm with their palm facing up.

2. Cross the victim's right arm over their body so that the back of their right hand rests on their left check.

3. Bend the victim's right knee to approximately 90 degrees.

4. Roll the person by grabbing their bent knee so that they are now laying on their left side.
