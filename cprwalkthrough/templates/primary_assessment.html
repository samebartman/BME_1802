CPR Walkthrough

Unsure of what to do after witnessing a possible cardiac arrest? Follow this step-by-step guide explaining what to do.

Check for Signs of Cardiac Arrest

1. Open kit and remove gloves, face shield and mat.

2. Determine if unresponsive by shouting, shaking, and rubbing their chest.

3. Determine if breathing by laying on their back, placing ear next to mouth and listening.

4. Call 911 or ask someone else to.
